Who We Are?

Welcome to AL-NOOR FABRICS, where quality, craftsmanship, and style come together. We are a leading fabric supplier that specializes in offering a wide range of premium textiles for every need — whether you’re a designer, a crafter, or someone looking for the perfect material for a special project. Based on decades of experience in the textile industry, AL-NOOR FABRICS is built on the values of quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction. At AL-NOOR FABRICS, we believe that the right fabric has the power to transform any creation, from everyday garments to couture masterpieces. Our collection is meticulously sourced, ensuring that each fabric is not only beautiful but also durable and easy to work with. We provide a vast selection of fabrics in various textures, patterns, and colors, designed to inspire your next fashion, interior, or DIY project


Our Story

Founded with a vision to make premium fabrics accessible to everyone, AL-NOOR FABRICS started as a small family business with a passion for textiles. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in the fabric industry. Our journey began with the simple idea of offering a curated selection of high-quality fabrics to our local community. But as demand for our products grew, we expanded our services online to cater to a global market.


Today, AL-NOOR FABRICS continues to stay true to its roots by providing personalized service, expert fabric knowledge, and a commitment to excellence. We are proud to serve a diverse range of customers, from hobbyists and seamstresses to professional designers and fashion houses
